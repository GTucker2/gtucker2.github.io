Experimenting with AI Mediocrity - Unity 3D Checkers

When I asked how I could implement a checkers AI, my dad, hovering nearby, asked "what if it was convincingly bad?" This is an attempt at that; a checkers AI which is believably terrible. Not one that throws its pieces recklessly in front of the oponent's, but one that makes convincingly bad plays. Alongside this little experiment I am also attempting to make a good checkers AI. I feel like some engineer somewhere would be rolling in their grave if I didn't.

For those who'd like to follow along more closely, I've opened a Git repo where folks can check out the source code as it develops. I'll also be posting updates as I progress. When I feel like things have reached a point where the project has become polished and interesting enough for anyone other than myself, I'll post a download link to the .exe as well.
