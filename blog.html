The monkey experiment;
an obligatory introduction.

As it says on my about page, howdy! I'm Griffin. To keep introductions short, I'll leave it to those who are interested to mosey on over to the about page for more general information on who I am, what I've done, and why you should love me now that I'm making my groundbreaking internet debut.

Now, you may be asking, what groundbreaking will I be doing? What should one even expect from this pastel-orange, Wes-Anderson-esque page that, to be honest, looks about as inspired as any artsy 20-something year-old's loosely-assembled Tumblr blog. Well, returning your honesty, I'll say that I'm not too sure. Part of me wants this blog to exhibit a sort-of stream-of-concious style. I could post about projects, research, momentary obsessions; you name it! I'd also like some of the content which ends up here to serve an educational purpose, if it could. Now that I'm out of University, there are a lot of things I'd that like to play around with; maybe my own documentation could help someone who's just as interested in the same things. At this point, all I can say for sure is that there's going to be a lot of experimentation, so just sit back and bear with me! It's going to be an alright time.

And like my dad once said (to be precise, today), "it's like the monkey experiment. Give him cigarettes if you want a novel in 60 days." I'll take my cigarettes coffee-in-a-jar style and we'll see if I can crank out something interesting in sixty days plus. Wish me luck.
